Privacy Policy - Mini Browser Platformer

Last updated: March 2026

What data do we collect?

Mini Browser Platformer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No accounts are required and no information is saved.

Multiplayer

The multiplayer feature uses PeerJS to create a direct connection between players. During this connection, your IP address is temporarily shared with PeerJS's servers solely to establish the connection. Once connected, all game data (player positions) is sent directly between players without passing through any server. No game data is stored anywhere.

Third party services

This extension uses PeerJS (peerjs.com) for multiplayer functionality. Please refer to their privacy policy at peerjs.com for more information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach out via the Chrome Web Store support page.
